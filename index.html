Travel Guide

Essential Travel Tips

Expert advice to make your journey smooth and memorable

Pack Smart

Pack light and smart. Roll your clothes to save space and always carry essential documents in your hand luggage.

Check Documents

Verify your passport validity, visa requirements, and travel insurance before booking your trip.

Budget Planning

Set a realistic budget including accommodation, food, activities, and emergency funds for unexpected expenses.

Stay Connected

Keep your devices charged, download offline maps, and inform family about your travel itinerary.

Weather Check

Check weather forecasts for your destination and pack accordingly to avoid any weather-related surprises.

Payment Options

Carry multiple payment methods including cash, cards, and digital wallets for different situations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a flight ticket?

You can book flight tickets by filling out the booking form on our website, calling us at +91-6156359772, or messaging us on WhatsApp at +91-9771648655. Our team will provide you with the best available options and prices.

Are you IATA certified?

Yes, Uma International Travel Services is an IATA certified travel agency, ensuring you receive reliable and professional travel booking services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, bank transfers, UPI, and credit/debit cards. Payment options will be discussed during the booking process.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Cancellation and modification policies vary depending on the service provider and ticket type. Please contact us directly, and we'll assist you with the cancellation or modification process and explain any applicable fees.

Do you provide travel insurance?

Yes, we can help you arrange travel insurance for your journey. Contact us for more details about available insurance options and coverage.

What are your office hours?

We offer 24/7 customer support via phone and WhatsApp. Our physical offices in Gopalganj have standard business hours, but you can reach us anytime for assistance.

Can you help with visa applications?

Yes, we provide visa assistance services. Contact us with your travel destination, and we'll guide you through the visa application process and required documentation.

Do you offer group travel discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates and discounts for group bookings. Contact us with your group size and travel requirements, and we'll provide you with the best available group rates.
